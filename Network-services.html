In contrast, IP PBX utilizes VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) technology to transmit voice data over IP networks, enabling integration with digital communication systems and reducing costs associated with long-distance calls.

Both systems offer features like call forwarding, voicemail, and meeting to discuss things calling, but IP PBX provides more benefits such as the ability to be made bigger or smaller, remote access, and combination of different things together that work as one unit with other IP-based services.
